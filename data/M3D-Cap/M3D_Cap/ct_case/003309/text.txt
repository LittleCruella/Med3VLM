Title:Dermoid cyst of the lacrimal gland


presentation:Soft tissue bulges in the temporal side of the right upper eyelid.


patient:
Age: 25 years

Gender: Female



discussion:The case illustrates the pathology-proved dermoid cyst within the palpebral part of the lacrimal gland with impression on and attachment with the related frontal bone zygomatic process.
